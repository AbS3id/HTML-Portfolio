My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kimi No Nawa

This is the 1st movie I watched for Jackie Chan, he made me fall in love with Kong Fu. It's a mixture of martial arts and funny scenes.

Mulan - 1998

This is my Favourite Disney Movie of all time. I've seen it a hundred times & still inspiring.
